# Unresolved-risk register policy

The normative register is `release/unresolved-risks.toml`. Every entry has a stable identifier, accountable owner, expiry, explicit disposition and release-blocking classification. The release gate rejects duplicate or incomplete entries and rejects any release-blocking risk.

Risks currently accepted for the software release candidate concern missing non-software field, authority and hardware evidence. They are not waivers and cannot support a field or production claim. Expiry requires reassessment; satisfying a risk requires immutable evidence and review through the applicable promotion authority rather than deleting history.
